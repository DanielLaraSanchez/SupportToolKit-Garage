SuPPort ToolKit-GARAGE

The Support ToolKit-Garage is an app designed to create a data base of Errors and Its possible solutions. This database will serve the main functionality of the upcoming app "The Support ToolKit". Such app is designed primarily to make the training of new members of the team a bit easier, by helping them to learn rapidly how to solve some of the most common issues that our clients experience with our products. And Who knows... In the future maybe It could also be used by them so they will only contact us when absolutely necessary.
